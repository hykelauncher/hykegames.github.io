WebGL Tower Defense
A little experiment with three.js. To make it more fun (and to stress your GPU), you can build towers even when you have no money.
This game is not balanced. Simply enjoy the flashy explosions. And the unnecessarily large files.
You're listening to the Halo Theme from the Halo 2 Soundtrack. (I did not create this epic song; please notify me if you need it removed.)
Send comments and complaints to devin@devinabbott.com

How to Play

To build a tower
Click the tower icon at the bottom of the screen. Then click the square you want to build on.

To upgrade / sell a tower
Click the square of a tower. Then choose up to upgrade or down to sell. Right click or click 'x' to cancel.
